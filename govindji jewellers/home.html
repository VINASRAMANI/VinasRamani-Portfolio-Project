Our Legacy

Govindji Jewellers
A Name You Trust

Govindji Jewellers is a trusted jewellery store in Surat offering gold, diamond, and bridal jewellery with beautiful craftsmanship and traditional designs.

Our journey began with a simple belief — that jewellery is more than an adornment; it is a memory, an emotion, and a legacy passed down through generations.

From elegant everyday gold pieces to elaborate bridal sets and dazzling diamond jewellery, every creation at Govindji Jewellers is crafted with precision and passion.

  • BIS Hallmarked Gold — All our gold jewellery is BIS hallmarked, ensuring purity and authenticity you can trust.
  • Certified Diamonds — Our diamonds come with international certification for quality and cut assurance.
  • Custom Design Service — Bring your vision to life with our bespoke jewellery design service.
  • Fair & Transparent Pricing — No hidden charges. We believe in honest, market-rate pricing.
  • Exchange & Buyback — We offer fair exchange and buyback on all gold jewellery purchased from us.
